Explore the Wonders of India

India is a land of diverse cultures, rich history, and breathtaking landscapes. From the majestic Himalayas in the north to the serene backwaters of Kerala in the south, India offers a plethora of experiences for every traveler. Discover ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and mouth-watering cuisine as you journey through this incredible country.

Kerala

Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals. Inland are the Western Ghats, mountains whose slopes support tea, coffee and spice plantations as well as wildlife. National parks like Eravikulam and Periyar, plus Wayanad and other sanctuaries, are home to elephants, langur monkeys and tigers.

Ooty

Cradled amidst the Nilgiris, Ooty is blessed with lush green forests, cascading waterfalls, serene lakes and sprawling tea plantations. You're sure to be enticed by the magnificent hills and mountains all around it.
